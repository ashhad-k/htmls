Forms

Powerful and Flexible Form Builder

Dynamic and Intelligent forms

Condition orientated forms, built from features such as hidden fields enables you to capture client data from form URL, cookies or post parameters. Set conditions to show or hide certain fields based on your criteria, add score to the client inputs to further integrate your business logic into the system.

partial and complete submission

Never lose a lead with the Loyica partial submission functionality:

Capture every piece of data provided by your potential leads, even when the form partially completed or not submitted at all. This data provides you with all the partial or fully submitted information from your forms and provides you with an overview of all the incoming information from your customers.

Client Entities

Manage your client jounery

Leads

This is where your leads and corresponding information about them are distributed. You can make notes, send e-mails or apply the sales intelligence forms to help manage your potential clients expectations.

Prospects

These are leads that have begun the account application/onboarding procedure. You can view the prospects application information, View KYC and AML results on an application by application basis in a neat and easy to read manner.

Clients

This is where your clients, who have completed the application process successfully will end up, with their associated trading account. From here you can manage your client relationship.

Reports

Create powerful custom reports on all the information that is gathered within your CRM.

Whether you are looking for Lead reports for sales managers, Operational reports on clients or marketing reports, the report generator enables you to report on your business in a simple manner with no need for complex rules.

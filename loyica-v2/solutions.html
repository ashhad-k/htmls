1. Interview

Our dedicated project managers work with stakeholders to identify business requirements.

2. Research

We begin any project with an analysis of strategic gaps, goals, and requirements, including competitor and business audits.

3. sitemap

We develop the high level site and software architecture that will guide wireframe design and prototype development

4. Prototype

We release a beta sample for the first round of concept testing by internal users to identify errors and improve functionality.

5. Moodboard

We turn concepts into a visual experience that allows the design aesthetic to be sensed in a meaningful way.

6. Design

We create the initial design structure, model, and aesthetic, including wireframe design

7. DEvelopment

We execute our strategy and design to create a single, easily integrated solution.

8. Testing

We test the software for bugs, version testing, and responsiveness across devices.

9. Launch

We’re on-site and hands on to deploy the project, and deliver immediate support for launch management.

10. support

We enable the next stage of tasks to enhance, improve, and and continuously deliver value.

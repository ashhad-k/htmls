X-Securities

overview

Based in the UK, X-Securities is a financial group that acquires FinTech startups harnessing the power of emergent technologies. X-Securities invests in firms that integrate a strong understanding of the Financial Services Industry with digital and tech innovation. The first project they worked with us for was their re-branding roll out and website launch.

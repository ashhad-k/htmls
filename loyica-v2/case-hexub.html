case study

Hexub

overview

Hexub is a UK based private equity group that invests in entrepreneurs building emerging technologies.

As a recently launched brand, they wanted to build there online presence as soon as possible to connect with their digitally focused market. To do this, they approached us to help launch a brand new site for their brand-new brand.

Challenges

A unique Niche

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

There aren’t many investors in the same niche as Hexub. Accordingly, we had to get creative to establish clear competitive research frameworks and benchmarks.

Uncharted Territory

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

While the team had long been experienced individual investors the brand was completely new. This included conceptualizing and executing brand content, identity, and guidelines, from the ground up.

A new demographic

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

While traditionally HeXub built their portfolio through their network and industry connections, they now sought to reach a new demographic. Our challenge was to analyze, target, and tailor the offering to, from scratch. .

Approach & Insight

initial analysis

Established values, an innovative outlook

While the HeXub team had long been active as experienced investors with a rich professional history, to cater to their new market of entrepreneurs their brand had to reflect the unique proposition of corporate experience and tech innovation.

Branding

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

Iconography

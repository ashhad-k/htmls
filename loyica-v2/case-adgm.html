case study

Abu Dhabhi Global Market

overview

Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) is an international financial centre located in Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the UAE and home to two of the world’s largest sovereign wealth funds. ADGM is a huge entity competing with the financial districts of London, New York, Singapore, and Dubai. However, they lacked a mobile app, a critical touchpoint necessary for a competitive advantage on a global scale.

Challenges

Brand repositioning

Brand repositioning

ADGM’s reputation as a financial center is well known. However, clients are less familiar with its up-scale dining destinations, high-end brand and boutique stores, and dedicated lifestyle services. Brand awareness is never a bad thing: but for this project, it did pose a challenge.

placeholder

Corporate finance brand, luxury aesthetic

ADGM is, first and foremost, a large-scale corporate hub, a a position that comes with a great level of gravitas. Our design and branding had to reflect this, while still reinforcing the luxurious mood and aesthetic that is part of the ADGM offering.

marketplace approach

Marketplace approach consulting

Our challenge was to integrate core business needs and functionalities, with a bespoke marketplace experience. At a technical level, this a design that could seamlessly provide everything from complex financial and regulatory information to a gallery shopping experience and concierge service.

Approach & Insight

initial analysis

One app, two user experiences

We needed to reflect ADGM’s two distinct propositions, and cater to two separate audiences:
B2C brands and B2B corporates, an approach we reflected across each stage of project strategy.

Competitor research

Competitor research

Our main competitors were world-class financial centers. To compete at this level, we had to take the best of what each competitor's mobile presence offered - and make it better by integrating ADGM’s unique lifestyle value proposition.

Wire framing and design

Wire framing and design

Given the two rich and distinct value propositions we were required to highlight, we couldn’t create a single user journey. To do this, we created two alternative wireframe designs, switchable within the app.

Development

Development

Our app had to be lightweight and user friendly, without compromising advanced tools and functionalities. These included search, GPS mapping, live financial market data, in-app contact capabilities, as well as AI based customizations and recommendations.

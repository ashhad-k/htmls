case study

Elezer

overview

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

Elezer ventures is an investment and advisory firm, offering marketing entrepreneurs the capital they need to grow.

Elezer invests in both traditional and digital marketing companies, and they approached us to create a site reflecting their love of classic design and innovative tech

Challenges

Launching a brand

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

Creating a brand from scratch is always a challenge. It’s even more difficult when the target audience is agencies, who will have a keen and critical viewer’s eye on everything - from design and development to content and copy.

User Testing

Smooth UX across a complex process

As the first phase implementation of the rebranding roll-out, the website was of critical importance to user testing. According, we had to embed appropriate response mechanisms and measurements to guide further strategy.

Crafting content

End-to-end, pan-industry consulting

Elezer’s existing content assets were about as creative as you could expect when it comes to corporate finance. We faced the ultimate challenge: taking dry regulatory, financial, and technical content - and making it come alive.

initial analysis

We knew it was imperative for Elezer’s wanted their brand identity to reflect their position as institutional investors, with an agency’s love of good design and smart digital marketing.

case study

Intelligic

overview

Intelligic is an investment and advisory firm that works with its partners to revolutionise learning and teaching through e-learning platforms and EduTech systems, for all stages ad ages of education.

While Intellegic’s counts various EduTech enterprises as part of its portfolio, their own digital platforms remained, for the moment, limited. We worked with them to begin their digital implementation strategy, beginning with the most important touchpoint: a website.

Challenges

Re-inventing an identity

New life to an established brand

Tearing apart and rebuilding an old brand is even harder than starting from scratch. We needed to ensure that while recreating an identity, nothing was lost of the original spirit.

Reposition corporate and advisory

More than just funding

While Intellegic’s team were well known as investors, they wanted to-reposition the Intellegic brand to move beyond simply funding to focus on their start-up mentoring and advisory offering

Balancing quantity with quality leads

Between inclusion and exclusivity

Intellegic has an inclusive vision encourages diverse ideas, at all stages and every geography. However, we needed to reinforce stringent quality control of Intellegic’s select partners

Approach & Insight

initial analysis

Complex Information Architecture. Intuitive UX

With the complexity and density of content, and our mandate to drive conversions, we knew our approach had to find a way to balance a complex information architecture with an intuitive UX.

case study

Fusion Market

overview

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

Fusion markets is a comprehensive trading service that offers both a state of the art trading platform and a wealth of knowlege, market insight, and support.

In short, they needed to customcreate and deliver an exceptional online platform, one that offered both high-end trading functionalities, and a smooth user and customer service experience.

Challenges

Differentiation

Differentiation

At the competitor research stage of the project, we came to a simple conclusion: trading platforms and sites more or less, look, feel and sound the same. Our challenge was to differentiate Fusion Markets while still remaining guided by industry best practices.

Conflicting User Journeys

Conflicting User Journeys

Trading platforms aren’t the easiest interfaces to deal with, and the two distinct user journeys of platform and site - with two different purposes- can become conflated. We needed to clean up often contradicting CTAs to organize a clear user journey.

Building Brand trust

Building Brand trust

When it comes to a platform on which people will be trading their money, affinity and trust is critical from a business, not just a brand perspective. Our content creation was guided by transparently addressing these needs, and a friendly approach.

Approach & Insight

initial analysis

Simplifying a complex exhange platform

We realized that while functionalities and features needed to remain guided by industry practices, our brand identity - including vision and visual identity, were our chance to differentiate FusionMarkets, and truly stand out.

Research

Research

In-depth industry and user research covering branding, interface functionality and consumer expectations for platform requirements and brand positioning.

Content

Content

Fusion market’s mission and values, and ethical, educational approach to trading is what sets it apart from simple trading platforms - and that’s the message we wanted to highlight in our content.

Design and Development

Design and Development

We undertook two phases of development: a site that’s designed for user experience and conversion, and custom building a trading platform with advanced capabilities, which can be easily scaled and customized for growth.

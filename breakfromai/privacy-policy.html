Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23rd 2024

BreakFromAI ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information we receive from you when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

BreakFromAI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data. The Extension operates entirely on your local machine and processes data locally.

Permissions

The Extension requires certain permissions to function correctly. Below are the permissions requested and their purposes:

  • activeTab: To interact with the current active tab and apply content filtering and blurring as per user settings.
  • storage: To save user settings such as keywords to filter and blur intensity locally on your machine, ensuring a personalized browsing experience.
  • tabs: To manage and apply settings across different tabs, ensuring consistent behavior and user experience.
  • host permissions: To access and modify content on all websites, enabling the filtering out of AI-related content as specified by the user.

How We Use Information

As the Extension does not collect or transmit any data, there is no information used by us for any purpose. All processing is performed locally on your device.

Data Security

We prioritize your data security. Since the Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any data, there are no security concerns related to personal data storage or transmission. All processing is done locally on your machine.

User Control

You have full control over the settings of the Extension. You can adjust the keywords for filtering, the blur intensity, and toggle the Extension on or off at any time. These settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted or shared.

Third-Party Services

BreakFromAI does not use or integrate any third-party services or APIs that collect or share your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@breakfromai.com

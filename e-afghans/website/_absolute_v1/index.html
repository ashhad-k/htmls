What is e-Afghans Portal?

The E-Afghans Portal is a secure, digital platform designed to provide Afghan citizens and foreign nationals with convenient access to consular services. It allows users to apply for various services, such as visas, passport renewals, and other official documents, entirely online.

The E-Afghans Portal makes it easier to access consular services by handling all requests online in one place.

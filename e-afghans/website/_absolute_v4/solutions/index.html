The Afghan Overseas Identification (AOID) is an official ID issued to Afghan nationals living abroad, allowing them to access consular services and prove their Afghan identity overseas.

Afghan nationals residing outside Afghanistan on a long-term or permanent basis are eligible to apply. Applicants must provide proof of Afghan nationality and residency abroad.

AOID is exclusively applied through the e-Afghan portal. Simply register an account, log in, and follow the instructions under “Apply for AOID.”

You can track your application status in real time through your account on the e-Afghan portal. Updates on your application’s progress will be visible in your dashboard under the “Service Request Summary” section.

If additional documents are required, you will be notified via the e-Afghan portal and by email. You can log in to the portal to upload the requested documents.

Once approved, you will receive both a digital and physical copy of your AOID. Your digital copy will be instantly available to you on the e-Afghan Portal while the delivery of the physical copy of your AOID will be mailed to you within 4 weeks upon approval.

Yes, the AOID has an expiration date, and renewals will be handled through the e-Afghan portal. Renewal requirements and instructions will be provided in your account prior to expiration.

Required Documents

Follow these steps to renew your passport through E-Afghan:

  • Passport

    Biometric page scan | 6+ Months Validity

    • Upload a clear scan of the biodata page.
    • Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your arrival date.
  • Photograph

    Recent color photo | White Background

    • High-resolution, color photo.
    • Plain white background and good lighting.
  • Identification

    National ID or Residence Permit

    • Clear copy of National Identity Card (CNIC/ID) or residence permit (if applicable).
  • Supporting Documents (Optional)

    Invitation Letter | Accommodation Proof

    • Invitation letter (if applicable).
    • Evidence of accommodation (hotel booking or host details).

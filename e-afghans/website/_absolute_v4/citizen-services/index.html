New Passport Application

Download Guide Apply for New Passport

Please read the information below carefully and make sure you are eligible before submitting an application.

Important:

Please check your eligibility first before proceeding for your online passport application via the E-Afghans Portal to avoid rejection.

For applicants 18 years old and above: If you do not have an existing Afghan passport, do not proceed with the online application. If this is your first time applying for an Afghan passport, you may apply in any Afghan Consulate near you.

Eligibility for New Passport Application

For applicants who are 18 years old and above:

  • You are an Afghan citizen.
  • You have an existing Afghan passport.
    • If you have an expired Afghan passport, you may proceed as long as it is in electronic format (not paper).
  • You are 18 years of age to be able to use the E-Afghans Citizen Portal.
  • You are 18 years of age to be able to apply for your dependents.
  • You give full consent to the terms and conditions of the application.
  • You must affix your true and correct signature on the application form.
  • You are willing to go through a basic identity check (Identity Verification) to confirm your details.
  • You do not have any legal restrictions, pending cases, or travel bans that may prevent passport issuance.
  • You are submitting all the required supporting documents.

For applicants who are below 18 years old and newborn:

  • You are an Afghan citizen.
  • You may apply with or without an existing Afghan passport (not required).
  • One of your parents should be able to apply for your new passport on your behalf via the E-Afghans Citizen Portal.
  • Your parents must give full consent to the terms and conditions of the application.
  • One of your parents must affix their true and correct signature on the application form on your behalf.
  • One of your parents should be willing to go through a basic identity (Identity Verification) check to confirm your details.
  • You do not have any legal restrictions, pending cases, or travel bans that may prevent passport issuance.
  • You are submitting all the required supporting document.
Documents Required for New Passport Application:

For applicant and dependents 18 years old and above:

  • Taskira (Front and Back) - If you have an expired Tazkira or does not possess any Tazkira, please present a clear copy of your Father’s or siblings’ Tazkira instead.
  • Existing Afghan Passport - An expired Afghan passport is accepted provided that it is in the electronic form (not paper).
  • Passport-sized Photograph - Ensure that you follow the standards of the photo for your passport application.
  • Residence ID - A copy of your Resident ID if you are an expat resident, or a copy of your university registration.

For dependent children less than 18 years old:

  • Taskira (Front and Back) - If you have an expired Tazkira or does not possess any Tazkira, please present a clear copy of your Father’s or siblings’ Tazkira instead.
  • Existing Afghan Passport - An expired Afghan passport is accepted provided that it is in the electronic form (not paper).
  • Passport-sized Photograph - Ensure that you follow the standards of the photo for your passport application.
  • Residence ID - A copy of your Resident ID if you are an expat resident, or a copy of your university registration.
  • Mother & Father passports - A clear copy of your parents’ passports.

For newborns:

  • Taskira (Front and Back) - If you have an expired Tazkira or does not possess any Tazkira, please present a clear copy of your Father’s or siblings’ Tazkira instead.
  • Existing Afghan Passport - An expired Afghan passport is accepted provided that it is in the electronic form (not paper).
  • Passport-sized Photograph - Ensure that you follow the standards of the photo for your passport application.
  • Residence ID - A copy of your Resident ID if you are an expat resident, or a copy of your university registration.
  • Mother & Father passports - A clear copy of your parents’ passports.
  • Birth Certificate
New Passport Application Process

Follow the below steps to apply for a new passport

Download Guide Apply for New Passport
  • 1
    Sign In or Create an Account

    Log in to your E-Afghan account or create a new account if you are a first-time user.

  • 2
    Access the Passport Services Section

    Once logged in, navigate to the "Passport Services" section under Citizen Services. Click “Apply Now” under New/Renewal Passport.

  • 3
    Complete a simple verification process (KYC check)

    Simply scan the QR code using your phone to perform the mandatory liveness check before proceeding to fill out the application form.

  • 4
    Fill Out the New Passport Application Form

    Complete the new passport application form with your correct details.

  • 5
    Upload Required Documents

    Upload clear, scanned copies of all required documents making sure that all are legible to avoid rejection of your application.

  • 6
    Proceed to Initial Fee Payment

    Pay the initial processing fees through our secure online payment system to proceed with your application processing.

  • 7
    Processing

    Once the initial payment is successful, you will receive a confirmation email that your application will be added to the queue for processing. Notifications will be sent to you at key stages of the process, including when the application is approved.

  • 8
    Approval & Remainder Fee Payment

    Once your application has been approved, pay the remainder fee to complete the final stages of your passport printing, issuance, and delivery.

  • 9
    Receive Your Passport

    Once approved, your renewed passport will be delivered to your registered mailing address.

Processing Time

  • Standard - 4-8 weeks
  • VIP - 5 business days (does not include delivery time)

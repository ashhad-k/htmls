About E-Afghans

E-Afghan is a centralized digital platform that offers Afghan citizens worldwide access to a range of consular services

Our platform is designed to streamline the consular process, making it easier, faster, and more secure for Afghan citizens to renew passports, apply for visas, request vital documents, and access emergency assistance—anytime, anywhere.

Our Mission

To provide Afghan citizens abroad with seamless, secure, and efficient access to consular services by leveraging cutting-edge technology. E-Afghan aims to simplify traditionally cumbersome processes like passport renewal, document requests, and visa applications, empowering citizens to manage their consular needs with ease.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Afghan citizens around the globe can connect with their consulates effortlessly.

Our goal is to create a unified, user-friendly platform that sets a new standard for consular services, while maintaining the highest standards of security, reliability, and transparency.

Why Choose E-Afghan?

Efficiency

Our fully digital platform reduces the time it takes to process consular requests by eliminating the need for in-person visits and manual paperwork.

Security

We prioritize your privacy by implementing the latest encryption and data protection technologies, ensuring your information is always secure.

24/7 Availablity

No matter where you are in the world, E-Afghan is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can request services, track applications, and receive updates at your convenience.

User Friendly

With a simple and intuitive interface, E-Afghan is designed for users of all backgrounds. You don’t need to be tech-savvy to access and manage your consular services online.

E-Afghan is more than just a service provider

Our Commitment

The E-Afghans Portal acts as a vital bridge between Afghan citizens and their consulates, simplifying the process of connecting virtually and managing requests online. By serving as a mediator, we ensure that citizens can easily submit and track their consular requests without the need for in-person visits.

We are committed to continuously updating the platform to streamline the online processing of requests and to digitize applications for greater convenience. Our goal is to provide an efficient, reliable, and always-available system, ensuring no downtime.

Authorized by the General Consulate of Afghanistan in Dubai, all processes and information provided through the E-Afghans Portal are official and based on their guidance.

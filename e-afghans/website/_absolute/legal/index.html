Introduction

E-Afghans Portal LLC ("we" "us" "our") is an authorised technology provider facilitating the digital access to consular services offered by the Consulate General of Afghanistan in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) (“Consulate”) for Afghan citizens abroad through our website, citizen portal and citizen mobile application (together the "Platform”). While we ensure secure and user-friendly technology and efficient service deliver, all policy decisions and application processing remain the exclusive authority of the Consulate, including payment processing relating to your applications.

We use cookies to enhance your experience on the Platform. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies?
  1. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use a mobile application. They help enable certain functionalities and remember your preferences to improve your user experience.
How We Use Cookies
  1. We use cookies specifically to:
    1. recognise your computer when you visit the Platform;
    2. retain users' email addresses and passwords when they log in to the Platform.
    3. track navigation through the Platform to personalise and enhance your experience;
    4. improve the usability of the Platform based on user behaviour;
    5. analyse the use of the Platform, including visitor traffic and patterns, to derive analytics and improve performance;
    6. optimise business logic and operational processes; and
    7. support the administration of the Platform.
Types of Cookies We Use
  1. We use the following types of cookies:
    1. Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Platform to function properly and provide essential services.
    2. Performance Cookies: Collect data about how you use the Platform to improve functionality and content.
    3. Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, settings, and choices to enhance user experience.
    4. Analytics Cookies: Help us monitor and analyse the performance and usage patterns of the Platform.
    5. Advertising Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
Your Choices
  1. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may significantly impact the functionality of the Platform. You may not be allowed to disable essential cookies required for the proper functioning of the Platform.
  2. If you choose to disable cookies:
    1. You may experience difficulties logging into the Platform, as login credentials and preferences may not be retained.
    2. Certain features of the Platform may not work as intended, including personalised content and navigation tracking.
    3. Your overall user experience may be reduced, and you may encounter errors or delays while interacting with the Platform.
  3. We recommend enabling cookies to ensure seamless functionality and an optimised experience on our Platform. Cookies help us deliver personalised services and continuously improve usability and performance.
Changes to This Cookie Policy
  1. We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to align with changes in technology, regulations, or operational practices. Updates will be posted on our Platform. Your continued use of the Platform after such changes are posted constitutes your express acceptance of such changes.
Contact Us
  1. For questions about this Cookie Policy, or how cookies are used, please contact us at admin@eafghans.com.

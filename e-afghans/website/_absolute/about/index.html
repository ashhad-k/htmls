About E-Afghans Portal

The E-Afghans Portal is an online platform that allows Afghan citizens from all over the world to access consular services digitally.

It makes it easier and more convenient for citizens by saving them time, effort, and money. Everything can be managed in one easy-to-use digital platform, from passport applications, accessing consulate support and more.

Our Mission

Our mission at E-Afghans Portal is to provide Afghan citizens worldwide with seamless, secure, and efficient access to consular services. We aim to save time and effort for our users while meeting their needs through a simple, digital platform that makes managing consular applications and requests more convenient.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a leader in online digital consular services, setting an example for other countries in digitizing solutions for consulate needs. We aim to make Afghanistan more competitive globally by implementing international standards for our services and continuously meeting the needs and expectations of our citizens.

E-Afghan is more than just a service provider

Our Commitment

The E-Afghans Portal acts as a vital bridge between Afghan citizens and their consulates, simplifying the process of connecting virtually and managing requests online. By serving as a mediator, we ensure that citizens can easily submit and track their consular requests without the need for in-person visits.

We are committed to continuously updating the platform to streamline the online processing of requests and to digitize applications for greater convenience. Our goal is to provide an efficient, reliable, and always-available system, ensuring no downtime.

Authorized by the General Consulate of Afghanistan in Dubai, all processes and information provided through the E-Afghans Portal are official and based on their guidance.

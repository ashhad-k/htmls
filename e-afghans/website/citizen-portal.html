Eligibility for Passport Renewal

Before proceeding, ensure that you meet the following conditions for passport renewal:

  • You are a current Afghan citizen with a valid or recently expired passport.
  • Your passport has expired or will expire within the next 12 months.
  • There are no changes required to your personal information (for example, a name change would require a separate process).
  • Your passport is not damaged or lost (in these cases, refer to our Lost Passport Services).
Documents Required for Renewal

To renew your passport, you will need the following documents:

  • Current Passport (scanned copy of the information page).
  • Recent Passport Photo (according to consulate photo guidelines).
  • Proof of Identity (Taskira/National ID).
  • Proof of Residency (utility bills or bank statements).
  • Payment for Renewal Fees (via secure online payment gateway).
Passport Renewal Process

Follow these steps to renew your passport through E-Afghan:

    Pay the renewal fee through our secure online payment system. You will receive a receipt for your payment, which you can track in your account.

    Submit Application

    Review your application for accuracy and submit it for processing. Once submitted, you can monitor the status of your application through your dashboard.

  • 7
    Track Your Application

    Receive real-time updates on the status of your passport renewal. Notifications will be sent to you at key stages of the process, including when the application is approved.

  • 8
    Receive Your Passport

    Once approved, your renewed passport will be sent to the consulate or embassy closest to your location for pick-up, or delivered to your address, depending on the options available.

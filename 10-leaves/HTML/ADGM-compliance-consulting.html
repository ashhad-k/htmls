ADGM Services

 

Compliance Consulting

Pre-authorisation

The FSRA focusses on compliance as being a key area of consideration during the process of evaluating an application for financial services. It mandates the appointment of a UAE-resident Compliance Officer (CO) and a Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO), and the creation of a robust compliance department at the firm.

 

Our services:

 
  • Provision of outsourced Compliance/MLRO officer
  • Compliance documentation, including manuals, compliance policies and compliance monitoring programs
  • Corporate Governance models, policies and procedures
  • Risk management policies and procedures
  • Establishment of your own internal compliance function
  • Training on FSRA Rulebooks and regulatory reporting
 

How we help:

 
  • Analysis of regulatory requirements relevant to your business, and advice on permissions required
  • Preparation of a focussed Regulatory Business Plan, that concentrates on the business proposition, the rationale, the mechanics of operations and the systems put into place to ensure sound compliance
  • Preparation of a detailed financial model for your business, with options for stress-testing
  • Advice on corporate governance and risk management, backed by assistance in developing a corporate governance code and other systems to ensure checks and balances
  • Preparation of all regulatory documentation including the compliance manual, the AML manual, and compliance monitoring programs.
  • Whether you are a start-up that needs a full suite of regulatory compliance documentation, or an established firm that needs to audit and review your existing documentation and provide you with assurance that your documentation is up to date and effective, we at 10 Leaves are able to guide you through the process.
 

Post-authorisation

 
  • Ongoing provision of outsourced Compliance officers
  • Compliance oversight
  • Compliance training
  • Compliance reviews and audits
 

WHY OUTSOURCE COMPLIANCE

 
Potential benefits of compliance outsourcing
The right compliance outsourcing provider should provide the following benefits:
  • Gains in efficiency and quality achieved by leveraging structured processes
  • Access to subject matter specialists
  • Seamless execution of end-to-end processes, from compliance assessment through corrective action
  • Flexibility to scale deployment of skilled resources up or down as needed
  • Data analytics and reporting tools that provide predictive trends and insights
  • Reduced burden on internal infrastructure and resources
  • Reduced cost
 

According to Deloitte :

 

A strategy of selective outsourcing — choosing which compliance processes to conduct in-house and which to outsource — can enable the organization to improve its allocation of resources. This reflects the overall goal of outsourcing — to place operational functions with a third party who can execute them at high levels of quality, with responsiveness, cost- effective delivery models, and to free up internal resources for revenue-generating activities.

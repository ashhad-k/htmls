our team
 
 

Rohit has over one and a half decades of experience in the region, with competencies in management & business consultancy, fund structures, management and administration, and regulatory compliances in multiple jurisdictions worldwide, including onshore in the United Arab Emirates and reputed offshore centers such as the Cayman Islands and Mauritius. He has consulted on establishing onshore regulated structures, including investment banks, private funds and advisory firms, in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) and the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM).

His entrepreneurial journey included establishing a fund administration company in Mauritius (exited in 2013) and establishing a joint venture that became the first entity to register at the then newly formed Abu Dhabi Global Market in 2015.

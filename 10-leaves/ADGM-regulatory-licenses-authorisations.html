ADGM Services

 

Regulatory Licenses and Authorisations

Entities that conduct financial services from the ADGM require authorizations from the Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA). Like any other onshore financial centre, the ADGM has a well structured road to authorisation. The process is detailed and requires a focused approach, comprehensive documentation and navigation through a host of rules and regulations. Financial services firms must demonstrate they have the systems and controls needed to manage regulatory risk, and checks and balances that are crucial for operating a financial services business.

 

What they look for:

 
  • The activities that you wish to conduct from the centre
  • A practical, viable and sustainable business proposition, with adequate capitalisation
  • Backed up by a thorough understanding of the underlying rules and regulations
  • An experienced and credible team
  • The checks and balances that form an integral part of any well-regulated financial business
 

How we help: :

 
  • Analysis of regulatory requirements relevant to your business, and advice on permissions required
  • Preparation of a focussed Regulatory Business Plan, that concentrates on the business proposition, the rationale, the mechanics of operations and the systems put into place to ensure sound compliance
  • Preparation of a detailed financial model for your business, with options for stress-testing
  • Advice on corporate governance and risk management, backed by assistance in developing a corporate governance code and other systems to ensure checks and balances
  • Preparation of all regulatory documentation including the compliance manual, the AML manual, and compliance monitoring programs.
  • Whether you are a start-up that needs a full suite of regulatory compliance documentation, or an established firm that needs to audit and review your existing documentation and provide you with assurance that your documentation is up to date and effective, we at 10 Leaves are able to guide you through the process.

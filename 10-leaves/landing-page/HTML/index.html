RECRUITMENT

Recruitment

Hiring is not about software, or databases. It is about people, and relationships. Sourcing talent involves a combination of multiple factors, but most importantly, it comes down to matchmaking. Ensuring that the company is as good a fit to the candidate, as is the candidate to the company and its existing employees.

LEARNING

Learning

Skill development is often construed as long-term degrees and gold-standard affiliations. While these are important to one’s career, we tend to concentrate on the skills that help employees contribute to their specific organisation and industry. Our learning solutions are tailored to your company – a mix of practical, short-term soft skill courses, and technical updates where necessary. Continuous learning is given priority, and access to interesting articles and snippets covering a wide range of topics, comes as a bonus

HUMAN RESOURCES

Human Resources

HR is about people skills. We care about people, and their alignment with the overall goals of the organisation.

This is why we start our engagements by speaking with people – staff and management. We listen to their goals, their aspirations, their values and objectives. We then design, implement and monitor bespoke policies and procedures to help them get to where they want to be. All this seamlessly, in a way that we become an integral part of our client companies.

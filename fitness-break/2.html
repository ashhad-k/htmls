OUR SPECIAL CLASSES

Take advantage of our fitness classes to tone your body, increase your flexibility, build strength, muscular and cardiovascular endurance, increase your metabolism and shred your body fat.

Functional training

involves training the body for the activities performed in your daily life

Plyo-cardio:

involves repeated rapid jumping and rebounding, to increase muscle power and strength

Circuit training:

It's a form of resistance training, using high intensity aerobics to build strength and muscular endurance.

Strongman training:

It's designed to stimulate your central nervous system for the ultimate strength, metabolic conditioning and Burning fat

Yoga-lates

fusion of yoga and Pilates that focus on flexibility and core

Tae-bo

involves cardio kick-boxing for fitness that focus on abs, clutes and thighs

Body toning bootcamp

involves variety of fitness exercises performed in circuits.

Dance for fitness

Fusion of Zumba, Salsa and hip-hop. Before you know it,you getting fit.

Yoga

involves strength flexibility, stretching, relaxation and meditation.

Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan (late)Founder

The firm was established by Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan (late), M.A., LL.B, who started practice in 1962. He is reckoned as a senior lawyer of the High Court of Sindh. His main practice areas were cooperative societies; constitutional matters; banking and finance; admiralty and shipping; family and service matters; general civil litigation. Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan was the founder chairman of the Citizen Cooperative Housing Society, Hyderabad and remained in the same position for 20 years.

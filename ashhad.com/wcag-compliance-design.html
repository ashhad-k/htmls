Inclusive Design Practices Accessible Design & Development

Crafting Inclusive Digital Experiences

I believe in creating digital experiences accessible to everyone. I follow WCAG guidelines to design and develop websites and digital assets that are usable and enjoyable for all users, regardless of their abilities.

View Design Portfolio

Color Contrast

I ensure that there is sufficient color contrast between the foreground and background to make content readable for all users, including those with visual impairments.

  • Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text
  • High contrast mode available

Descriptive Links and Headings

My links are visually distinct, descriptive, and concise. Headings are logical and nested to help users navigate the site easily.

  • Clear and descriptive link text
  • Logical heading structure

Alt Text for Images

All non-text content, such as images and graphics, have alt text descriptions to ensure that screen readers can interpret them accurately.

  • Alt text for all images
  • Descriptive alt text for complex graphics

Keyboard Navigation and Focus Order

The site is designed to be navigable using only a keyboard, with a logical focus order and clear focus indicators.

  • Logical focus order
  • Clear focus indicators

Error Prevention and Correction

I provide feedback for input errors and offer suggestions for correction to ensure users can complete forms accurately.

  • Error feedback and suggestions
  • Reversible, checked, or confirmed submissions

ABOUT INVESY

We’re looking for the architects
of a digital economy

We invest in consultancies that deliver intelligent solutions and digital ecosystems to drive industry innovation and exceptional client-service. We pride ourselves on identifying partners with the potential to introduce high-value, game-changing engagements in the field of corporate consulting.

Our investments strategy is focused on 3 areas:

Legal Tech

We look for partners who digitize regulatory processes, from monitoring and reporting to compliance. We’re particularly interested in businesses incorporating the use of AI technologies.

RegTech

We look for consultants and companies with technologies focused on legal solutions and services. We are interested in tools that include, but are not limited to, legal matter management, document assembly, electronic discovery, and legal accounting.

Digital Transformation

We look for companies driving digital transformation, across corporate functionalities. We seek partners who design creative new solutions rather than those supporting traditional methods.

Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan

The firm was established by Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan (late), M.A., LL.B, who started practice in 1962. He is reckoned as a senior lawyer of the High Court of Sindh. His main practice areas were cooperative societies; constitutional matters; banking and finance; admiralty and shipping; family and service matters; general civil litigation. Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan was the founder chairman of the Citizen Cooperative Housing Society, Hyderabad and remained in the same position for 20 years. Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan is considered as leading lawyer of the country on cooperative societies matters. Besides law Mr. Aziz Khan was a literary figure and was widely known in the literary circles. Mr. Aziz Khan was also remained actively involved in handling Constitutional pro bono matters of general public interest. Mr. Aziz Khan like all successful men always believed in human competence and self integrity and honour. His life can be well described in the following lines:

The man of life upright,
 Whose guiltless heart is free,
From all dishonest deeds
 Or thought of vanity.

The man whose silent days
In harmless joys are spent,
Whom hopes cannot delude
Nor sorrow discontent:

That man needs neither towers
Nor armour for defence,
Nor secret vaults to fly
 From thunder’s violence.

He only can behold
 With unaffrighted eyes
The horrors of the deep
And terrors of the skies.

Thus scorning all the cares
 That fate or fortune brings,
He makes the heaven his book,
 His wisdom heavenly things;

Good thoughts his only friends,
 His wealth a well-spent age,
 The earth his sober inn
 And quiet pilgrimage.

Thomas Campion (1567-1620)

Our company

AZIZ LAW ASSOCIATES INTL., is one of the fastest growing law firm of Pakistan based in Karachi Pakistan with regional/international representation having a modest over 50 years of experience in attending cases of complex nature. Today following the same rules Aziz Law Associates Intl., has become a distinguished and reputed name in the legal circles.

Mission

At Aziz Law Associates our lawyers handle both contentious and non-contentious matters. Like all successful legends including Walt Disney, Winston Churchill, Abraham Lincoln and Mohammad Ali Jinnah, our founder Mr. Abdul Aziz Khan, Senior Advocate, focused on human competence for achieving the desired goals of our esteemed clients and self generated integrity to assure secrecy of their privileged information.

Easy accessibility

The canvas of our firm has widened with our affiliated offices in Abu Dhabi and Dubai as Abdul Azeez Al Ameri, Advocates and Legal Consultants. Aziz Law Associates Intl., is proud to announce opening a European Union Office in Warsaw, Poland recently handling immigration practice for Europe, Australia, Canada and UK,.

In-depth Analysis



Our firm provides in depth analysis with specialized knowledge of legal issues to resolve complex matters and disputes. In order to save healthy business relationship and goodwill of our esteemed clients from damaging in courts we emphasis on ADR’s and mediation as a progressive legal tool to solve problems.

As a comparatively smaller/boutique law firm we assure personalized attention to our clients and easy accessibility through all modes of communication on the tip of our clients’ fingers. Contrary to quantity we believe in quality of work and that truly matters to our clients.

At present Aziz Law Associates Intl., represents our clients in UK, USA, Canada, UAE and Malaysia. At Aziz Law we offer our clients options of fixed (lump sum), contingency, part-payment, and blended fees arrangements. Depending upon the nature and stake involved, we share in the risk of litigation by sharing financial burden of our clients. Once our interest are aligned with our clients we are both working towards common objectives.


Litigation can be an expensive and time consuming diversion from running your business or attaining your desired result in shortest possible time. We counsel our clients with legal understanding of the issues and result oriented solutions of their legal problems.


It is generally said that “the minute your read something that you cannot understand, you can almost be sure that it was drawn up by a lawyer” (Will Rogers) At Aziz Law Associates we work contrary to this perception and put all possible endeavors to help our clients find the desired solutions of their legal problems through legal assistance and with their coordination.

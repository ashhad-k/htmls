Taxis are the most common form of public transport in Afghanistan, especially in larger cities like Kabul, Herat, and Mazar-i-Sharif. You can easily find both shared and private taxis. Itâ€™s recommended to negotiate the fare beforehand, as meters are often not used.

Bus

Afghanistan has a network of local and intercity buses connecting major cities and towns. City buses are a budget-friendly way to travel within Kabul, while long-distance buses link cities like Kandahar, Herat, and Jalalabad.

Rickshaw

Terms and Conditions of this Application
  1. This application process is intended for walk-in clients only.

  2. Please ensure all information provided is complete and accurate. Submission of incorrect or fraudulent information will result in automatic rejection.

  4. The application fee must be paid within 30 minutes of receiving the payment link. Failure to do so will result in automatic rejection of your application.

  7. Payments are accepted only through the official payment link (card payment).

  8. Please ensure you provide a valid and working email address to receive the payment link and further instructions. If the email does not appear in your inbox, please be sure to check your spam or junk folder.

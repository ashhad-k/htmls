Instructions

The online passport application process is presently available exclusively to individuals residing in specific regions including (Europe, the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada and Australia) are subject to certain criteria.
Eligibility criteria for the online passport application:

  • Possession of a valid previous machine readable (electronic) passport
  • A recent colored passport size photo with a plain white background

For children under the age of 7 without having machine-readable (electronic) passport, the following documentation are required:

  • Birth certificate
  • Parents valid machine-readable (electronic) passports
  • Parents official marriage certificate

Please ensure that all information provided in this form is accurate and complete, as it will be used in a legal document. Any inaccuracies or omissions may have legal consequences.

Additionally, you are permitted to apply only on behalf of your immediate family members and dependents.

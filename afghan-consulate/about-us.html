Our Vision, Mission & Goals

Vision

To rebrand Afghanistan by means of traditional cultural, sports and economic diplomacy, turning the mission in Dubai into an exemplary diplomatic model in the region.

Vision

To protect the interests of the State and citizens of Afghanistan, both individuals and institutions, in Dubai and the Northern Emirates of the UAE.

Goals

Under the light of the Afghan government’s foreign policy, With due consideration to all the statutory laws and regulations in the UAE, and in coordination with the Afghan Embassy in Abu Dhabi

  • to promote the development of social, commercial, economic, cultural, and scientific relations between the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan and the UAE,
  • to enhance the role and responsibility of the mission in the host country,
  • to improve the performance and quality of the consular services provided by the mission,
  • to introduce more transparency and accountability,
  • to facilit ate customers’ access to the consular services, thus winning the trust and appreciation of the visitors.
Activities
  • Diplomatic
  • Institutional contacts
  • Cultural
  • Admin./financial
  • Public relations

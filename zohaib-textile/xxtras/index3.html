Welcome to ZohaibTex

We are an ISO 9001-2015 certified Yarn and Home Textiles manufacturing and exporting company located in the port city of Karachi, Pakistan.

Our firm was established over 3 decades ago and due to our hard work and exemplary performance we are now one of the leading exporters in the country for our product categories.

We do customized manufacturing and labeling for our customers across the globe and would be glad to cater to your requirements as well. You can reach us through our Contact Us form and we will revert promptly.

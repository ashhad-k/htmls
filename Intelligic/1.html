Our Story

We're a group of established professionals with a portfolio of education-related investments.

In 2014, we noticed that while investors across sectors were rapidly diversifying their portfolios to reflect a focus on online platforms and emerging technologies , there was no such corresponding trend within the education sector.

We resolved to fill what we saw as a critical gap in the market.

We believed that there was no area of investments more satisfying than education.

And when it comes to shaping minds, we believed the impact of technology would be significant, and truly meaningful.

Based on this belief, we came together to found ____, and work on a single mission: harnessing the power of technology to revolutionise education.

Our Mission

We aim to support the development of entrepreneurial ventures dedicated to what is arguably the most important demographic: students today who will be leaders tomorrow.

We believe that students of all ages deserve the tools and resources that can help them succeed. Our ambitions are simple, and threefold.

We’re on a mission to:

  • Enhance teaching
  • Enable learning
  • Expand educational reach

Our Mandate

We believe the best way to stimulate the market for rapid and positive change is to invest in technologies that improve educational content and delivery through EduTech systems or e-learning platforms.

We invest in platforms that enable educators to deliver - and allow students to access - new methods and resources for learning.

We believe that with the right early stage capital and strategic guidance, education entrepreneurs have the prowess to radically enhance teaching, enable better learning, and improve the overall educational experience.

Our Portfolio

We believe that early stage companies allow us to the opportunity to contribute our expertise, not just our capital.

We also believe that small and ambitious enterprises present the greatest potential for radical impact.

Accordingly, we are focused on identifying, investing in, and nurturing companies at the following stages:

  • Seed
  • Early stage
  • Emerging growth

Our Criteria

The diverse companies in our portfolio all have one thing in common: they each demonstrate an improved quality of education through the use of online platforms.

While each potential partner is evaluated on their unique merits, we look for businesses with a stand-out value proposition, powerful platform, and robust business model.

In addition, we look at five key criteria when selecting a partner:

  • Innovation of the idea
  • Scalability of the model and platform
  • Capabilities of the technology
  • Ambition and talent of the team
  • Alignment with our goals and values

Our Partners

We invest in people, not just companies.

The single most important factor in selecting ventures is the team, not just the business we invest in.

We believe in transforming education and investing in human capital.

To enable the massive change we envision, we need people who think big: problem solvers, dreamers, out of the box thinkers.

People with the capability - and passion - to reimagine education and a goal, like ours, to create a better learning experience for all students of all ages.

We build trusting, collaborative, and honest relationships with our partners, making it crucial that we’re aligned on values and passionate about delivering on our common goals.

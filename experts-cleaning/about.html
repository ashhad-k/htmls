About us

Experts Corner Cleaning Services, a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Expert Global Group of UAE, was established with a vision to provide unparalleled cleaning & maintenance services to local corporate sector.

Having developed and experienced in the field of commercial maintenance, cleaning and overall management, Experts Corner Cleaning Services has emerged in every aspect, as one of the most reliable cleaning solutions provider in UAE.

To fulfil the increasing demand of this fast growing industry, we ensure the influx of highly trained and dedicated team of professionals equipped with the essential technical expertise.

At Experts Corner Cleaning Services, we believe that the well-being of individuals and the efficiency of systems are conducive to the productivity and the quality of the surroundings we maintain. Therefore, we strive hard to offer excellent cleaning & maintenance services and solutions to meet the increasingly challenging needs of our clients.

Being a client centric company, our client satisfaction is the utmost priority for us. We are at work 24/7, so that our clients get continuous servicing wherever and whenever required. We also care for and take complete responsibility of the property/premises of our clients that we are entrusted with. We at Experts Corner Cleaning Services has acquired the International Quality Certifications to streamline our processes and procedures; such as QRS ISO 9001:2008 in Quality Management Systems.
